Canigao Island

A tropical gem off the coast of Matalom, Canigao Island is a small yet captivating island known for its clear turquoise waters, coral reefs, and laid-back atmosphere. With its mix of natural beauty and relaxing coastal vibes, it offers visitors a perfect destination for swimming, snorkeling, and unwinding by the sea.

Know More:

Located just a short boat ride from the mainland, Canigao Island is popular among both locals and tourists seeking a peaceful island escape. The island is surrounded by vibrant coral reefs, making it an excellent spot for snorkeling and marine exploration. Its shallow waters and sandy shores also make it ideal for casual swimming and beachside relaxation.
